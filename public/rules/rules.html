Rules

Controls

WASD - Move

LCLICK - Shoot

SPACE - Heal (requries max mana)

Death

Upon reaching 0 hp, you will respawn at your initial spawn location 3 seconds later.

Goal

Capture gems deployed in the middle. At 10 gems, a timer will deploy. You must hold on to your 10 gems without losing them to death in order to win.

Have fun.
